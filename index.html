The Problem

"The SEC calls. Your AI agent made 10,000 trades last quarter. Can you explain a single one?"

This is not hypothetical. It is happening right now.

  • AI Agents Are Deciding at Scale: Agents trade, lend, and score risk faster than humans can supervise.
  • No One Can Explain Why: Every compliance tool today was built for human decisions. They log the output—not the reasoning. When regulators ask 'why,' no one has an answer.
  • Regulators Are Coming Now: SEC & FINRA now classify AI as supervised technology. FINRA 24-09 mandates traceable AI decisions.

The Solution

The Black Box Recorder for AI Agents in Finance

Lyslle captures, archives, and replays the full reasoning context of every AI agent decision in a SEC/WORM-compliant graph. It is the flight recorder that proves exactly why every decision was made.

01 Capture

Real-time decision telemetry embedded in the agent execution path. Captures reasoning chains, policy evaluations, and overrides as they happen.

02 Archive

WORM-compliant temporal graph with cryptographic signing. Every decision becomes an immutable, SEC 17a-4 compliant artifact.

03 Replay

Full state reconstruction at any past moment. Regulators replay any decision exactly as it occurred—reasoning, environment, and policy state.

How It Works

Four Steps. Zero Disruption.

1. Agent Executes: AI agent makes a decision in trading, risk, or compliance.
2. Lyslle Intercepts: Embedded in execution path—zero latency, captures full decision context.
3. Context Graph Archived: Signed, timestamped, written to immutable WORM graph.
4. Audit Replay Available: Any decision fully reconstructed at any future moment.
